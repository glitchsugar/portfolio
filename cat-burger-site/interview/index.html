JW interviews Ro

On Cat Burger

On Monday, March 22, 2021, JW interviewed Ro about the object Cat Burger, the subject of their focus throughout the Spring 2021 semester of Design.

JW: How did you acquire cat burger?

Ro: I got him on a road trip, at a gas station, at, like, 2 AM, and just, I had to have it!

JW: What kind of material is it made from? Is it the kind of material that when you press it it rises back slowly or...?

Ro: I'm pretty sure it's foam! When you squish it it does take a while to fully re-inflate? Refill? I don't know, but it does it fairly quickly, just takes a hot second

JW: Is the foam itself colored or is there, like, a layer of paint coating it or something like that?

Ro: I'm not entirely sure. Cat burger is chipped in some areas and it's a milky yellow type color under that, but under other areas you can see the orange coming through so maybe it's painted but in several layers? The only way to be sure is to cut open cat burger and it's not worth it.

JW: Have you seen a design like cat burger anywhere before? Like in shows or online or anywhere or is it a unique character?

Ro: Until this project I hadn't really seen another cat burger until I googled it and found a bunch of websites selling them!
